# LiveMark Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 14, 2026

> **Plain-language summary:** LiveMark is designed as a local-first Markdown editor. Your documents are edited and stored on your device unless you choose to export them, move them, or open content outside the app.

## 1. Who We Are

LiveMark is a desktop Markdown editor focused on local editing and export functionality. This Privacy Policy explains how LiveMark handles information when you use the app.

## 2. Information LiveMark Processes

LiveMark may process the following information on your device in order to provide its features:

- **Documents you open, create, edit, and save**, including Markdown text and related file content.
- **Images you insert or paste** into documents, when you choose to do so.
- **App preferences**, such as theme, editor settings, font size, and similar local preferences.
- **File and folder paths** needed to open files, save files, show a sidebar file tree, and restore your work state.

## 3. Data Collection

**We do not collect personal data from LiveMark users through our own servers.**

LiveMark does not require account creation, does not include in-app advertising, and does not sell your personal information. LiveMark is intended to work locally on your device.

## 4. Tracking

**LiveMark does not track you across apps or websites.**

LiveMark does not use third-party tracking SDKs for advertising or cross-app tracking.

## 5. Analytics, Diagnostics, and Crash Reports

LiveMark does **not** automatically send analytics, usage telemetry, or crash reports to our servers.

If you choose to contact us for support or feedback, you may voluntarily send information by email. In some cases, LiveMark may ask for your permission before attaching a bug report or diagnostic information to help investigate a problem. Any such report is sent only with your action and permission.

## 6. How Your Data Is Used

Any information processed by LiveMark is used only to provide app functionality, such as:

- displaying and editing Markdown content;
- saving files you choose to save;
- exporting documents to formats you choose, such as HTML or PDF;
- remembering local preferences and restoring app state on your device.

## 7. Storage and Retention

Documents and preferences are stored locally on your device or in locations you choose. LiveMark does not maintain a cloud copy of your documents through our own service.

You control retention by editing, moving, or deleting your files and, where applicable, clearing app data on your device.

## 8. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share your information with third parties for advertising or tracking purposes.

Information may leave the app only when *you* choose an action such as exporting a file, opening a link, printing, copying content to the clipboard, using operating-system features or third-party apps outside LiveMark, or sending us feedback or a bug report by email.

## 9. Internet Access and External Content

LiveMark may allow you to open links contained in your documents using your device's default browser or operating system. When you open an external website or service, that third party's terms and privacy policy apply.

## 10. Children's Privacy

LiveMark is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 11. Security

Because LiveMark is primarily local-first, the security of your data depends in part on the security of your device, operating system, and the locations where you choose to store your files. We encourage users to keep their devices updated and protected with appropriate security controls.

## 12. International Users

Because LiveMark does not operate a user-content cloud service, there is generally no routine transfer of your document data to our servers in other countries.

## 13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated version with a new effective date.
